One Stop Shopper
The online in-store stock/price checker

One Stop Shopper is a website that features a store locator, inventory checker, and price checker all in one website. This website was created to help those who are impacted by COVID-19 and cannot find the products they are looking for, such as cleaning supplies, hand sanitizer, paper towels, and more.
